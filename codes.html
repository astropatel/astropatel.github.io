Twinkle

I am working on a Python spectral energy distribution characterization (SED) code. Basically, figure out the temperature and rough radius properties of a star from measured fluxes, as well as characterize any excess flux from dust. Check it out at my github repository: www.github.com/astropatel/twinkle. FYI: Still under development.
